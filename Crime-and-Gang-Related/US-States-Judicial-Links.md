# US States Judicial Links
Each state has their own judicial (criminal, and civil) court databases. Some a linked together, and others aren't. This page is for finding those systems broken down by state.




# Indiana
- [Odyssey Public Access - MyCase](https://public.courts.in.gov/mycase/#/vw/Search) - https://public.courts.in.gov/mycase/#/vw/Search
> Searching criminal & citation, civil, family, probate, appellate, and commercial cases.
